Formula Reference

SUVAT Equations

\(v = u + at\)

\(s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2\)

\(s = vt - \frac{1}{2}at^2\)

\(v^2 = u^2 + 2as\)

\(s = \frac{(u + v)t}{2}\)

Newton's Laws

\(F = ma\)

\(W = mg\)

Friction: \(F_f = \mu R\)

Inclined plane: \(mg\sin\theta\)

Momentum & Impulse

\(p = mv\)

\(J = \Delta p = mv - mu\)

\(J = Ft\)

Conservation: \(m_1u_1 + m_2u_2 = m_1v_1 + m_2v_2\)

Calculus Relations

\(v = \frac{dx}{dt}\)

\(a = \frac{dv}{dt} = \frac{d^2x}{dt^2}\)

\(a = v\frac{dv}{dx}\)

\(x = \int v \, dt\)

Projectile Motion

Horizontal: \(x = v\cos\theta \cdot t\)

Vertical: \(y = v\sin\theta \cdot t - \frac{1}{2}gt^2\)

Max height: \(H = \frac{v^2\sin^2\theta}{2g}\)

Range: \(R = \frac{v^2\sin 2\theta}{g}\)

Energy & Work

KE: \(\frac{1}{2}mv^2\)

PE: \(mgh\)

Work: \(W = Fs\cos\theta\)

Power: \(P = \frac{W}{t} = Fv\)
